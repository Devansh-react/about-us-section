National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar has been a pioneering educational institute for over 30 years and has been conferred the title of Institute of National Importance (INI) by the parliament of India. The cultural cavalcade of NIT Jalandhar, UTKANSH the three day blast is a medley of entertainment, creativity, competitions, enthusiasm and everything else one can expect.

UTKANSH'S HISTORY

The annual cultural fest “UTKANSH”, which attracts thousands of students across the country to participate in the days of extravaganza comprising of competitions, seminars, symposium and workshops. Since its inception in 2006, this festival has been identified as a convergence of cultures and a confluence of best talents across the country.
